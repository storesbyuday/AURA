The Process

How to Order

Ordering a custom resin piece is simple. Here's exactly how it works from start to finish.

01
Choose & Enquire

Browse the collection above or DM on Instagram. Tell us what product you want and any personalization details — names, photos, dates, colors.

02
Confirm & Pay

We'll send you a quote. Once confirmed, a small advance secures your order. Payment via e-transfer, UPI, or agreed method.

03
Craft & Preview

Your piece is handcrafted with care. We share a preview before finalizing — your satisfaction comes first.

04
Deliver with Love

Pickup available in Winnipeg or we'll arrange shipping. Your piece arrives beautifully packaged, ready to gift or keep.
